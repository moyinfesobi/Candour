Understanding how to support

Just as we learn how to respond in cases of medical emergencies, it is of utmost priority that we learn about how to support people facing online harassment. Community support has been proven to be one of the most effective ways of combatting the effects of online harassment. We are a people powered community that provides direct support to people facing online harassment

Sign Up

An occurrence of online harassment can be mind-boggling. It often leaves victims irritated, frightened, uneasy and a number of other feelings. It can also lead to physical symptoms like insomnia, headaches and panic attacks. In situations like this, it is important to take a step back to care for yourself. One important fact to remember is that online harassment is not your fault. It is normal to feel frightened, but you should understand that being harassed is not your fault and you are not alone. It is very important to take time to recover and take care of yourself.

You are not to blame
